Here is the timeline of my life, highlighting the most important events in 10 steps.

In 2023, I was promoted to president of my student organization. This role allowed me to refine my leadership skills, manage larger teams, and coordinate more complex projects, preparing me for future professional challenges.

In 2024, I started my internship at Epicor, where I learned Angular in just three weeks and began working on a project with my team. This experience enhanced my technical skills and taught me the importance of teamwork and continuous learning in a professional environment.

Also in 2024, I concluded my role as president of my student group to focus entirely on my future as a developer. This decision marked a significant step towards my professional goals, allowing me to dedicate more time to my career development.
